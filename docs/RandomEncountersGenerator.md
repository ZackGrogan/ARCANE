# Random Encounters Generator

## Overview
The Random Encounters Generator is a feature in the ARCANE application that allows Dungeon Masters to create dynamic and engaging encounters for D&D 5e campaigns using AI. This tool generates encounters with rich narratives, customizable elements, and integrates seamlessly with D&D 5e rules and lore.

## Features
- **AI-Generated Encounters**: Automatically generate encounters with detailed descriptions, monster tactics, and environmental settings.
- **Customizable Elements**: Modify generated encounters to fit your campaign needs.
- **Difficulty Scaling**: Calculate encounter difficulty based on party size and levels.
- **Integration with D&D 5e API**: Fetch monster stats, items, and spells to enhance encounters.

## How to Use
1. **Access the Encounter Generator**: Navigate to the Encounter Generator section in the ARCANE application.
2. **Input a Prompt**: Describe the type of encounter you want to generate.
3. **Generate Encounter**: Click the generate button to create an encounter.
4. **Customize and Save**: Edit the generated encounter details and save it for future use.

## Best Practices
- Use clear and descriptive prompts to guide the AI in generating relevant encounters.
- Review generated content for accuracy and alignment with your campaign.
- Utilize the customization options to tailor encounters to your party's needs.

## Troubleshooting
- If the generator fails to create an encounter, check your internet connection and try again.
- Ensure prompts are detailed enough to provide context for the AI.
